About Us

JETRIDERS CLUB PAMPANGA INC. is a motorcycle riding organization promoting safety, brotherhood, discipline and responsible riding.

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 GMM meetings
  • Join 3 consecutive rides
  • 155cc scooter and above
  • Registered motorcycle
  • With side mirrors
  • Complete riding gear
