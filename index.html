About Us

JETRIDERS CLUB PAMPANGA INC. is a motorcycle riding organization promoting brotherhood, discipline, safety, camaraderie, community service, and responsible riding.

Established: 2001

SEC Registration No.: CN201720885

Location: Pampanga, Philippines

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 General Membership Meetings
  • Join 3 consecutive rides
  • 155cc scooter and above
  • Registered motorcycle in good condition
  • Side mirrors required
  • Complete riding gear required during rides
  • Respect club rules, marshals, and fellow riders
