About Us

Established: 2001

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 General Membership Meetings.
  • Join 3 consecutive rides.
  • Motorcycle must be registered and in good condition.
  • Side mirrors are required.
  • Complete riding gear is required during rides.
  • Minimum displacement: 155cc scooter and above.
Probationary members must comply with meeting, ride, motorcycle, and safety requirements before approval as official members.
