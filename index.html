About Us

JETRIDERS CLUB PAMPANGA INC. is a motorcycle riding organization promoting brotherhood, discipline, safety, camaraderie and responsible riding.

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 General Membership Meetings
  • Join 3 Consecutive Rides
  • 155cc Scooter and Above
  • Registered Motorcycle
  • Good Running Condition
  • Side Mirrors Required
  • Complete Riding Gear
  • Observe Club Rules and Regulations
