About Us

JETRIDERS CLUB PAMPANGA INC. is a motorcycle riding organization promoting safety, brotherhood, discipline, camaraderie, service, and responsible riding.

Established: 2001

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 General Membership Meetings
  • Join 3 consecutive rides
  • 155cc scooter and above
  • Registered motorcycle
  • Motorcycle must be in good condition
  • Side mirrors required
  • Complete riding gear during rides
