About Us

JETRIDERS CLUB PAMPANGA INC. is a motorcycle riding organization promoting brotherhood, safety, discipline, camaraderie, and responsible riding.

Established: 2001

SEC Registration No.: CN201720885

Club Highlights

🏍️ Established 2001

👥 30 Official Members

🏆 25+ Years of Brotherhood

📍 Pampanga, Philippines

📅 Monthly Meeting: Every 2nd Saturday of the Month

Membership Requirements

  • Attend 3 General Membership Meetings
  • Join 3 consecutive rides
  • 155cc scooter and above
  • Registered motorcycle
  • With side mirrors
  • Complete riding gear during rides
